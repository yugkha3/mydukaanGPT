How can I change my business address?
Learn how to add or change your business address
N
Written by Namrata Sawhney
Updated over a week ago

To change or update your business address, head over to the Account section by clicking the dropdown on your Dukaan web dashboard.

In the Store details section, scroll down till you see the Store Address details. Click on it to update the address.

A Google maps popup will open. You can enter your manually or select the location.

Click on the Save address button and the address will be saved.
