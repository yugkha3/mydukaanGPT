  • Head Category: Enter the category to which your product belongs.

  • Variant SKU ID: Enter the SKU Id of the variant of the product.

  • Variant weight: Enter the weight of the variant of the product.

  • Variant Final Selling Price: Enter the final selling price of the product.

  • Variant Display Price: Enter the MRP of the product.

  • Variant weight unit: Choose the appropriate weight unit for the product from the drop-down menu.

  • Product GST/Tax Value ( Percentage): Add the tax percentage you will be charging for the product.

  • EAN/HSN Code: Enter the EAN/HSN code of the product.

  • SEO title: Enter an SEO-optimized meta title for your product.

  • SEO description: Enter an SEO-optimized meta description for your product.

    • Note Entering data in the SEO categories will enable the SEO plugin and add the values accordingly
