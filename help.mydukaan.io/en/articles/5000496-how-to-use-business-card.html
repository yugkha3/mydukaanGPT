How to use Business Card?
Learn how to share your store's digital business card
N
Written by Namrata Sawhney
Updated over a week ago

A business card is a highly personal form of marketing. It contains all the important information and helps you market your business, all in seconds.

Note: Learn how to edit and design your own Business Cards.

To get your digital business card, click on Manage. Now choose Promotional Designs and select Business Card. You can see many pre-built business cards just for you.

Select the design which works best for your business and click on Download this card button.

Your business card will be downloaded and now you can share this card directly with your contacts.
