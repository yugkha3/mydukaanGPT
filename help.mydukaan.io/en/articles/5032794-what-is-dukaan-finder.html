What is Dukaan Finder?
Now search your nearby Dukaan stores across categories and order from them easily

Updated over a week ago

Ever wanted to search for local Dukaan stores near you? We’ve made that just as easy for you.

With Dukaan Finder, you can search for any store anywhere in India across different categories like Kirana, Fruits & Vegetables, Restaurants, Electronics, and many more.

To get started, go to https://mydukaan.io/finder. You’ll see the below screen. This is where you have to select a location and category for which you want to find stores.

For example, you want to search for Kirana Stores in Mumbai. In location, put Mumbai and choose Kirana, FMCG & Grocery in the category and click on the search button.

You’ll see tons of stores in the selected category and location from where you can now order easily.

Now, it’s your turn to find local stores near you. 😇
